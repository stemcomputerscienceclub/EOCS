Competition Overview

EOCS is Egypt's premier team-based competition in computational science, challenging students to solve real-world scientific problems using computational methods and creative thinking.

Our Vision

Innovation

Foster innovative thinking in computational science by challenging students to solve complex scientific problems using modern computational methods.

Collaboration

Promote teamwork and knowledge sharing among Egypt's brightest young minds in science and computing.

Education

Bridge the gap between traditional science education and modern computational methods through hands-on experience.
