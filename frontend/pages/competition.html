Competition Structure

The Egyptian Olympiad in Computational Science is structured to provide participants with a comprehensive experience in developing innovative solutions. Our competition follows a carefully designed process that ensures fair evaluation and maximum learning opportunities.

Registration Phase

Teams register and submit their initial proposals. Each team must consist of 2-4 members.

  • Team formation
  • Project proposal submission
  • Initial screening

Development Phase

Selected teams work on their projects with mentorship and guidance from industry experts.

  • Project development
  • Mentorship sessions
  • Progress tracking

Submission Phase

Teams submit their final projects, including documentation and presentation materials.

  • Final submission
  • Documentation review
  • Technical assessment

Judging Phase

Projects are evaluated by a panel of experts based on innovation, technical merit, and impact.

  • Project presentation
  • Technical evaluation
  • Winner selection

Competition Rules

To ensure fair competition and high-quality submissions, we have established comprehensive rules and guidelines. Please review them carefully before participating.

Team Requirements

  • 2-4 members per team
  • All members must be students
  • One team leader designated
  • Cross-institution teams allowed

Project Guidelines

  • Original work only
  • Must solve real-world problems
  • Use of open-source tools encouraged
  • Documentation required

Submission Requirements

  • Complete source code
  • Technical documentation
  • Project presentation
  • Demo video

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation (30%)
  • Technical merit (30%)
  • Impact (20%)
  • Presentation (20%)

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I form a team?

Teams can be formed through our platform. You can either create a team and invite members or join an existing team. Each team must have 2-4 members.

What happens if a team member drops out?

Teams can replace members during the registration phase. During the development phase, replacements require approval from the organizing committee.

Can I participate in multiple categories?

No, each team can only participate in one category. However, you can form different teams for different categories.

What happens after winning?

Winners receive their prizes at the awards ceremony and may be invited to showcase their projects at various tech events and conferences.
