To ensure the highest scientific quality, EOCS collaborates with university professors specializing in computational science across various domains.

  • Problem set review
  • Quality assurance
  • Difficulty calibration

Judging Process

Quality Control

  • Error identification
  • Problem correction
  • Content updates
  • Final verification

Key Responsibilities

Content Development

Design and review competition problems, ensuring they meet educational standards and challenge participants appropriately.

Academic Guidance

Provide expert guidance on computational methods, scientific accuracy, and problem-solving approaches.

Evaluation

Assess solutions based on innovation, scientific accuracy, algorithmic efficiency, and presentation quality.

Feedback

Provide constructive feedback to participants, helping them improve their computational science skills.
