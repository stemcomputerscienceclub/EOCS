Eligibility Requirements

Learn about who can participate in EOCS and how teams are formed. The competition is open to both high school and undergraduate students.

Competition Categories

Category A: High School

  • Students in grades 9-12
  • Any high school in Egypt
  • Basic programming knowledge
  • Strong math/science background
Perfect for students interested in computational science and looking to challenge themselves.

Category B: Undergraduate

  • University students (Years 1-4)
  • Any Egyptian university
  • Programming experience required
  • Advanced STEM knowledge
Ideal for university students ready to apply their knowledge to real scientific challenges.

Team Formation

Form Your Own Team

  • 3-4 members per team
  • Same category members only
  • All members must qualify
  • One team per student

Join as Individual

  • Take qualification exam
  • Get matched with a team
  • Meet other qualifiers
  • Form new connections

Additional Requirements

Documentation

  • Valid student ID
  • School/University enrollment proof
  • Parent consent (if under 18)

Location

  • Residing in Egypt
  • Available for onsite finals
  • Internet access for online rounds

Timing

  • Meet registration deadline
  • Commit to all competition rounds
  • Attend team meetings
